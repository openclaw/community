# Rules (Mirror)

> Mirror of the public rules listed in the Discord server.

1. Do not spam. Do not also make the moderators define spam, play nice. This includes recruitment posts and stupid jokes and comments for the sake of trolling people.

2. No age-restricted or obscene content. This includes text, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or other graphically disturbing content.

3. Treat everyone with respect. Absolutely no harassment, witch hunting, sexism, racism, or hate speech will be tolerated. Because of people's lack of ability to be civil, no politics are allowed here either.

4. If you see something against the rules or something that makes you feel unsafe, let staff know with #report. We want this server to be a welcoming space!

5. No crypto, betting or trading talk. Ever.

6. Need help? #help and #users-helping-users are here for you. Do not post support questions in #general. Questions about models? #models

7. A lot of questions are answered in our docs: https://docs.openclaw.ai/start/faq - Fastest way to be unblocked? Switch to git install, start claude/codex in that folder: `curl -fsSL https://openclaw.ai/install.sh | bash -s -- --install-method git`

8. No advertising or self-promotion (server invites, "Check out my project", "anyone need a developer", etc) outside of the #self-promotion channel without permission from a staff member. This includes DMing fellow members.

9. No lobster, butter, or cooking jokes. A Clawd has feelings too!

## Our Community Staff can moderate how they see fit. If you have problems, go to #report.
