## Team map (quick)

- **Admin**: final decisions, escalations, staffing.
- **Discord Moderator (text)**: general rule enforcement in text channels.
- **VC Moderator (voice)**: voice channels and VC events.
- **Helper**: support channels (#help, #users-helping-users, #models).
- **Configurator**: permissions, bots, automod, server config.

## Access + setup

- Confirm you have the correct team role(s).
- Join your team channel and the shared staff channel.
- If you are a Trial Moderator (<@&1474489940279820339>), ask your lead for first tasks.

## Tooling overview

- **Barnacle**: all moderation actions (no native Discord moderation tools).
- **Audrey**: voice automod and `/report-vc` logging.
- **Kiai**: message/voice tracking (used by leads and senior staff).
- **Answer Overflow**: #help management and indexing.
- **Craig**: voice recording for events.
- **ChannelBot**: Configurator utility bot.
- **Krill**: support bot in #help/#ideas (ping Shadow if it goes offline).

## Operating norms

- Appeals are handled by someone other than the originating mod. Use https://appeal.gg/clawd.
- Use `/say` commands for redirects (help channels, models channel, docs/FAQ).
- Standardize common punishments using the [Incident Playbook](incident-playbook.md).

## First week checklist

- Shadow a lead or senior mod for a few cases.
- Handle at least one low-severity case with a lead available.
- Review the Incident Playbook (self-promo, crypto, doxxing, VC incidents).
- Practice `/warn`, `/mute`, `/ban`, and `/caselist`.
- Ask your lead what coverage windows are most needed.

## Guardrails

- When in doubt, mute for 1 hour with the reason "pending moderation" and ask for help.
- If you are unsure, ping online moderators in #vc-mods or #moderators.
- If you’re doing a voice moderation, use `/report-vc` whenever possible.
- Do not use native Discord moderation tools.

## Communication + activity

- Stay reasonably active (chat presence, VC presence, support answers, mod discussions).
- Post inactivity/LOA notices in your team channel and ping your lead.
- LOA format:
  - Dates: 12th June 2024 - 1st July 2024
  - Reason: Optional (DM a lead/Admin if you prefer)
